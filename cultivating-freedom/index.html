We invite you to participate in Cultivating Freedom, where you design new ways of thinking, being and doing for a future that’s calling you.

Free Yourself From Historical Habits of Mind

Our habits build up over a lifetime of experiences. Mostly invisible to us, these habits operate without conscious thought on our part. And yet, our habits shape how we perceive and respond to our world, from how we hold our creativity, to how we respond to our colleagues and those closest to us. You’ll learn to identify and boldly let go of the old ways of thinking that no longer serve you or your business.

Shift the view of your awareness

Even when we recognize the momentum our habits have, it can be difficult to see how to change that pattern. We help you learn how to situate the context of your choices and position your decisions differently.

Respond in new ways to challenging situations

Letting go of habitual thinking, and shifting the focus of your awareness then allows you to experience a spaciousness, opening up new pathways in your thinking so that what is truly new can emerge. You’ll develop awareness of the thinking behind your thinking, building a capacity for multiple, non-dual perspectives.
