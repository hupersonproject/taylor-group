About the Course

Business basics are continually evolving. The basics you learned earlier in your career gave you a foundation, but they operate in a continually changing context.

To stay clear on the viability and resilience of your business in the face of changing conditions, every business leader needs to revisit fundamental questions periodically.

Join us to explore what you already know from a fresh perspective, integrate your insights into new contexts, and stay current with essential business practices as they evolve into the future.

We accomplish these goals by providing you with an in-depth assessment tool that will allow you to gain a better understanding of the fundamentals of your business, including the current state of your operations, staff, and finances. We will review your responses and tailor the course to ensure we speak to your concerns over the ensuing 4 sessions, offered via Zoom.

Our experienced course designers and facilitators draw from years of experience with their clients, and from working with future trends. Our time together includes a focus on:

  • Cashflow: aligning your cashflow and budgeting with your business goals and current capabilities
  • Brand Identity: getting clear on your brand identity, and its articulation in the market Employee
  • Retention: attracting and retaining the right employees to achieve your business goals
  • Value to customers: making sure you are consistently delivering value to your customers

In this Course

You’ll emerge from this course experience with a grounding in the essential concerns of business leaders, how to articulate goals and priorities to your team, and how to apply first principles to your business operations.

Move into 2024 with confidence, an improved ability to navigate the challenges and opportunities, and a clear sense of the fundamentals of your business blueprint, giving you the tools to create an inspiring business case for 2024 and beyond.
