Drafting Your Business Blueprint

Business basics are continually evolving. The basics you learned earlier in your career gave you a foundation, but they operate in a continually changing context.

To ensure the viability and resilience of your business in the face of changing conditions, every business leader needs to revisit fundamental questions.

Join us to explore your current assumptions from a fresh perspective, integrate your insights into new contexts, and stay current with essential business practices as they evolve into the future. Fulfillment and success happens in the journey of being present and aware of all aspects of your business.

Work with an in-depth business assessment, and gain a better understanding of the current state of your business, including your operations, staff, and finances. As part of the course, you will get a 1-on-1 session with a coach to review the assessment. You’ll gain clarity around what is important to your business now, and access to greater possibilities for your future contribution.

  • Cashflow: aligning your cashflow and budgeting with your business goals and current capabilities
  • Brand Identity: getting clear on your brand identity, and its articulation in the market
  • Employee Retention: attracting and retaining the right employees to achieve your business goals
  • Value to customers: making sure you are consistently delivering value to your customers

In this Course

You’ll emerge from this course experience with a grounding in the essential concerns of business leaders, how to articulate goals and priorities to your team, and how to apply first principles to your business operations.

Move into 2024 with confidence, an improved ability to navigate the challenges and opportunities and a clear sense of the fundamentals of your business blueprint, giving you the tools to create an inspiring business case for 2024 and beyond.

Commonly Asked Questions

Who is this workshop series for?

This workshop is for entrepreneurs and leaders in small to medium sized businesses. If you are a leader who feels like day-to-day issues in your business are pulling your attention away from what you really want to work on, or simply want raise your professional sightlines, this course is for you.

Who Are The Coaches?

This course is lead by executive coach Liam Mullaney. He was inspired to create it by patterns he and his colleagues observed over 10 years of working with individual clients. Several other Taylor Group coaches and partners contributed to the design of the course, and will be participating in its delivery.

What Are The Expected Outcomes? / What Will I Learn?

The modules of the course focus on creating a healthy cashflow, hiring and retaining employees, and articulating your brand identity. Throughout the course we will be exploring ways you can connect the disparate parts of your business into a cohesive whole.

What happens if I can’t make a session?

This course was designed to create maximum value in a short series of sessions, so we strongly encourage you to attend all 4 group sessions and your individual business assessment overview. If you have a concern about missing a session, reach out to us and we can look for ways to make sure you don’t miss out on anything.
